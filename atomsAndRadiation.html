Electrons, Protons and Neutrons

the size of the nucleus is roughly 10,000 times smaller than the atom itself

Atoms are made up of three sub-atomic particles:

  • Electrons
  • Protons
  • Neutrons

The following table summeries the relative masses and charges of the particles

Sub-atomic particle Relative charge Relative mass
Electron -1 1/1836
Proton +1 1
Neutron 0 1

These are all relative values. for charge the elementry charge e (which is the charge of an electron and proton) is -1.6*10-19 Coulombs. For mass the mass of a proton 1.6*10-27kg and for an electron it is 9.1*10-31kg.

The Atom

Atoms are electrically neutral as they have the same number of protons as electrons. The negative charges of the electrons balance the positive charges of the protons.
